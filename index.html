This website is intended to be a place to showcase my development knowledge, it contains the various technical projects I've been involved in as well as personal info about me.

Who am I?

My name is Jake Hall and I'm an aspiring student at Bridgwater and Taunton College taking a Level 3 Diploma in Applied Computing and Software Development, as well as a part time Tier 2 IT support technician. I currently live in a small town just outside Taunton called Norton Fitzwarren, located within Somerset in the UK.

All my life I have had a passion for computers and creating things, hence my current aim is to work as a developer. I'm currently unsure as to what area of development I want to focus on, so I have dabbled in a wide range of different programming languages. You can see this reflected in my projects; I have used "backend" languages such as C# and VB.net as well as web based languages such as CSS, JavaScript and Jquery.
